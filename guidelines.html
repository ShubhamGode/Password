GUIDELINES FOR STRONG PASSWORD




  1. Longer passwords are generally stronger. Aim for at least 16 characters, but longer is better.
  2. Use a mix of different character types:
    • Uppercase letters (A-Z)
    • Lowercase letters (a-z)
    • Numbers (0-9)
    • Special symbols (e.g., !, @, #, $, %, ^, &, *)
  3. Create passwords that are unique to each account and not based on easily discoverable personal information
  4. Stay informed about best practices for online security, as threats evolve over time.
